About Us

Delhi Public School Howrah Library is a comprehensive online library hosted on the local server of Delhi Public School Howrah. Our aim is to provide students and faculty with easy access to a wide range of books and educational resources.

We believe that reading is an essential part of learning and personal growth. Our online library offers a vast collection of books covering various subjects, genres, and interests. Whether you are looking for academic textbooks, fiction novels, or reference materials, our library has something for everyone.

Our user-friendly interface allows you to search for books by name, author, or publisher. Once you find a book of interest, you can explore its details and availability. While our library primarily serves the Delhi Public School Howrah community, we strive to create a culture of knowledge sharing and extend our resources to anyone within the local network.

We are committed to continuously expanding our collection and providing a seamless experience for allusers. We welcome your feedback and suggestions to improve our library and make it a valuable resource for all.

Thank you for choosing Delhi Public School Howrah Library. Happy reading!
